Memorize

Practice memorizing text

Welcome! This tool is for you to practice memorizing text of your choice via interactive activities.

How do I use this?

Enter the text you would like to memorize.
Select an activity to interactively practice the text content.

Practicing Now

When you have selected an activity, it will be available for you to practice here.



When you are done, check your answer to see feedback.

Techniques for memorization, drawn from educational pedagogy and cognitive science.

Feel free to sample and see what works for you.
